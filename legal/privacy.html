What we collect today

APEX currently collects:

  • Waitlist email addresses: voluntarily provided when you join the waitlist. Used only to notify you when paid tiers launch.
  • Analytics data: basic anonymized usage metrics via Netlify.
  • Local browser storage: watchlists, alerts, and signal history stored in your browser's localStorage. This data is on your device only; APEX does not have access to it unless you explicitly sync it (future feature).

What we don't collect

  • We do not sell your data.
  • We do not share your email with third parties.
  • We do not track your trading activity.
  • We do not have access to your broker account.
  • We do not have access to your financial information.

Third-party services

APEX uses the following third-party services that may process your data:

  • Netlify: hosting and serverless functions
  • Anthropic (Claude): AI chat and analysis generation. Prompt content sent to Anthropic may include your current chart state.
  • Alpaca: market data
  • Finnhub, FRED: news, fundamentals, macro data
  • Supabase (future): user account and subscription data
  • Stripe (future): payment processing

Cookies

APEX currently uses minimal cookies, primarily for session management and Netlify analytics. A full cookie policy (including consent banner for EU visitors) will be published with Termly.
