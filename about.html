About

Built for traders who take it seriously.

Tradolux is an AI-powered trading terminal built for independent retail traders who want institutional-grade analysis without the institutional price tag.

Why we built this

Most retail traders are flying blind. They have charts, but no analysis. They have data, but no context. They have platforms built for order routing, not for thinking.

Tradolux changes that. Five AI agents — Chart Intelligence, Sentiment, Fundamental, Risk Management, and Macro — analyze every ticker simultaneously and give you a complete read before you commit to a position.

What we believe

  • Analysis should happen before the trade, not after.
  • AI should augment your judgment, not replace it.
  • Every trader deserves the same quality of analysis as a hedge fund desk.
  • Simplicity is a feature. Complexity is a bug.

The product today

Tradolux is a single-page trading terminal deployable in any browser. Real-time IEX market data. Five AI agents powered by Claude. Nine smart signal detectors. Multi-timeframe, multi-symbol chart layouts. Backtester. Performance tracking. All in one tab.

What Tradolux is not

Tradolux is not a broker. We don't execute trades, hold funds, or touch your money. Your trades happen at your broker of choice; Tradolux provides the analytical layer.

Tradolux is not a registered investment adviser. Nothing on the platform constitutes personalized investment advice. We provide general market information and AI-generated analysis for educational and informational purposes only. You are responsible for your own decisions.
