FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about Tradolux, its AI agents, signals, and pricing.

General
What is Tradolux?+
Tradolux is an AI-powered capital markets intelligence platform. Five learning agents generate real-time signals, analyze market conditions, and improve their accuracy over time through a closed-loop evaluation system.
Is Tradolux a broker?+
No. Tradolux is an intelligence and signal platform. You execute trades through your own brokerage account. We never hold your funds.
Who is Tradolux for?+
Active traders and investors who want institutional-grade AI analysis without institutional prices. Whether you trade stocks, ETFs, or are exploring options, Tradolux gives you the intelligence layer that was previously only available to professional desks.
What markets does Tradolux cover?+
US equities and ETFs with live data. Crypto and options coverage is on the roadmap.
Is my data secure?+
Yes. Tradolux uses Supabase-powered authentication with row-level security. We never store brokerage credentials, API keys, or trading account information.
Signals
How are signals generated?+
Five AI agents — Chart, Sentiment, Fundamental, Risk, and Macro — analyze each symbol simultaneously. A signal fires when multiple agents reach consensus, giving you multi-layered confirmation rather than a single-indicator alert.
Are signals real-time?+
Yes. Signals update continuously during market hours as the agents process incoming price and volume data.
How accurate are the signals?+
Accuracy is tracked live. Every signal is evaluated against real price outcomes after 10 bars on its timeframe. Win rates and R:R ratios are published in the platform's agent performance dashboard.
What is the HFT Shield?+
A microstructure analysis tool that evaluates real-time order flow patterns to detect when institutional players are active. This level of data is typically reserved for hedge funds and professional trading desks.
Can I set custom alerts?+
Yes. Price alerts, percentage move alerts, volume spike alerts, and more. Free trial: 3 alerts. Pro: 20 alerts. Elite: unlimited.
AI Agents
What are the 5 AI agents?+
Chart (technical pattern recognition), Sentiment (market mood and crowd positioning), Fundamental (business quality and earnings momentum), Risk (volatility and position safety), Macro (Fed policy and sector rotation).
Do the agents learn over time?+
Yes. Every signal is evaluated against real price outcomes. Agents automatically adjust their confidence weights based on accuracy — small changes apply automatically, large changes (20% or more) go to admin approval before applying.
Can I see each agent's accuracy?+
Yes. The agent performance dashboard shows per-agent direction win rates, R:R win rates, average P&L, and a 30-day rolling accuracy chart for each agent.
What is the Macro Intelligence agent?+
It monitors FRED economic data, Federal Reserve communications, yield curve shape, sector rotation, and DXY strength to give every trade a macroeconomic context. It classifies the current macro regime and identifies which sectors have tailwinds.
