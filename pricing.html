FAQ

Common questions.

Can I cancel during the trial?

Yes. Cancel any time before day 14 and you will not be charged. No questions asked.

Can I change plans later?

Yes. Upgrade or downgrade any time. Proration is handled automatically by Stripe.

What is annual billing?

Annual plans are billed in full at the start of the billing period at approximately 20% off the monthly rate.

Is there a refund policy?

If you cancel during your 14-day trial you are not charged. After the trial, charges are non-refundable but you can cancel to stop future billing.

Is Tradolux a broker?

No. Tradolux is an analytical platform. We do not execute trades, hold funds, or provide investment advice. Trades happen at your broker of choice.

Is Tradolux a registered investment adviser?

No. Tradolux provides general market information and AI-generated analysis for educational and informational purposes only. Nothing on the platform constitutes personalized investment advice.
